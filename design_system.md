# LuxeBook Design System

The design system is engineered for the ultra-high-net-worth individual, evoking the hushed exclusivity of a private concierge. It balances the technical precision of high-end horology with the organic, serene atmosphere of a luxury resort.

The aesthetic is **Sophisticated Minimalism with Glassmorphic Depth**. It utilizes expansive negative space to signify "luxury of space," while incorporating subtle tactile elements that feel high-touch and bespoke.

---

## Colors

- **Primary Emerald Green:** `#0F5E4D` (Primary actions, growth, stability)
- **Secondary Burgundy:** `#6D213C` (Premium accents, membership CTAs)
- **Tertiary Rich Gold:** `#D4AF37` (Borders, highlights, selected states)
- **Warm Ivory:** `#F8F5F0` (Foundational canvas)
- **Santal Blush:** `#E8C9B0` (Surface shifts, secondary containers)
- **Deep Charcoal:** `#1E1E1E` (High-contrast typography)

---

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (Editorial, authoritative serif)
- **Body & Labels:** Inter (Functional clarity for booking and tables)
- **Label-Caps:** Inter (Capitalized, letter-spaced for metadata)

---

## Elevation & Depth

1. **Base Layer:** Warm Ivory (`#F8F5F0`) matte finish.
2. **Floating Layer:** Semi-transparent white (80% opacity) with 20px backdrop blur.
3. **Shadows:** Soft, elongated charcoal shadows at 5-8% opacity with 30px+ blur radius.
4. **Borders:** Hairline borders (1px) in Rich Gold (`#D4AF37`) at 20% opacity.

---

## Shapes

- **Inputs / Buttons:** 8px (`0.5rem`) for standard controls.
- **Cards & Containers:** 24px (`1.5rem`) for primary rhythm.
- **Large Modals / Banners:** 32px+ for fluid layout.
